I LOVE THE BACKSEAT LOVERS!!!

Biography:

The Backseat Lovers is an absolutely amazing band that was founded in a small Utah suburb in 2018 when four insanely talented musicians came together and started making delicious tunes together. With 3,173,005 monthly listeners on Spotify and a specialty in Indie/ALT Rock, The Backseat Lovers gain new fans everyday and continue to make music that means so much to listerners like myself.

Band Members:

  • Josh Harmon - Guitar, Vocals
  • Jonas Swanson - Guitar, Vocals
  • Juice Welch - Drums, Vocals
  • KJ Ward - Bass Guitar
